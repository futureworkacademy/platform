## SCENE 3: What It Is (0:35 - 1:05)

**[VISUAL: UI walkthrough - dashboard, briefing, decisions]**

**NARRATION:**
"The Future of Work is an 8-week business simulation where students become executives at Apex Manufacturing - a mid-sized company facing the same AI adoption challenges your students will encounter in their careers.

Each week, students receive intelligence briefings - press releases, internal memos, stakeholder interviews. Then they make strategic decisions: How much to invest in AI? How to handle workforce anxiety? How to communicate with the union?

And here's what's different: Every response is evaluated by AI using a transparent 4-criterion rubric. Students see exactly how they're scored on Strategic Thinking, Stakeholder Awareness, Risk Assessment, and Communication Quality."

---

## SCENE 4: Academic Integrity (1:05 - 1:35)

**[VISUAL: Rubric displayed, then side-by-side comparison]**

**NARRATION:**
"Let's address AI and academic integrity directly.

Students can't simply copy-paste prompts into ChatGPT. Each week's decisions require synthesizing context-specific briefing materials they just read. Generic AI outputs fail our rubric.

After each deadline, students see anonymized top responses from their peers. Authentic work is recognizable. And because we show the rubric upfront, students learn what excellent strategic thinking looks like - not just how to avoid detection."

---

## SCENE 5: Dual Scoring (1:35 - 1:55)

**[VISUAL: Dashboard with financial AND cultural health scores]**

**NARRATION:**
"Most simulations optimize for one metric: profit. We score two: Financial Performance AND Cultural Health.

Students learn that sustainable leadership requires both. Cut corners on workforce morale? Watch your cultural health score tank. And unlike real life, they can try again next week with a different strategy."
