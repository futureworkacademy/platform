# Future Work Academy
## Workforce Training for Iowa's Manufacturing Future

---

### The Challenge

Iowa manufacturers face a critical skills gap:

- **$32 billion** GDP goal requires Industry 4.0 adoption
- Supervisors lack training in **AI/automation decision-making**
- Workers fear displacement; managers struggle to **communicate change**
- Traditional training doesn't build lasting **decision-making skills**

---

### The Solution

**Future Work Academy** is an immersive business simulation where manufacturing supervisors and managers practice leading through technology transitions—before they face them in real life.

**How It Works:**

| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 1-8 | Participants step into an executive role at a fictional manufacturing company |
| Weekly | Make strategic decisions on automation, workforce, and stakeholder management |
| Ongoing | Receive AI-powered feedback on decision quality and rationale |
| End | Compete via leaderboards; earn completion certificates |

**8 weeks. 1 hour per week. Measurable results.**

---

### 260F Eligible Training

Future Work Academy qualifies as a **260F Iowa Jobs Training Program** expense:

- **Enhances employee performance** in technology transition leadership
- **Delivered through community college partnership** (your institution)
- **Clear cost documentation** via per-seat licensing
- **Measurable outcomes** with built-in assessment and completion tracking
- **Reimbursable training activity** under Iowa Code 260F
